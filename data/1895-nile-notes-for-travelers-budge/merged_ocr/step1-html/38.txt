<br/>
Seti I. conquered the rebellious tribes in Western<br/>
Asia, and built the Memnonium at Abydos. He<br/>
was famous as a builder, and attended with great<br/>
care to the material welfare of his kingdom. He<br/>
is said to have built a canal from the Nile to the<br/>
Red Sea.<br/>
<br/>
Rameses II. <span style="color: #0000FF;">undertook </span><span style="color: #0000FF;">many </span>warlike expeditions, and<br/>
brought <span style="color: #0000FF;">Nubia</span>, Abyssinia, and Mesopotamia under<br/>

liberal patron of the arts and sciences<span style="color: #0000FF;">, </span>learned<br/>
men like <span style="color: #ff0000;">Pentaurt </span>were attached to his court. He<br/>
