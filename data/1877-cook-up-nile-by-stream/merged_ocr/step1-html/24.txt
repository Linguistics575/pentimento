SEVENTEENTH DAY.<br/>
<br/>
<span style="color: #0000FF;">Descend </span>from Luxor to <span style="color: #ff0000;">Bellianeh</span>, where donkeys have been<br/>
telegraphed for from Luxor.<br/>
<br/>
EIGHTEENTH DAY.<br/>
The excursion to Abydos is now made-a good two hours' ride,<br/>
through rich fields; at about 12 <span style="color: #0000FF;">o'elock </span>we are again on board<br/>
and the journey is resumed.<br/>
<br/>
NINETEENTH AND TWENTIETH DAYS.<br/>
<br/>
Continue the voyage without stopping, except at such places<br/>
as were not visited according to Programme during the up<br/>
journey; the Steamers only stop to coal, or for provisions,<br/>
according to requirements<span style="color: #ff0000;">.<br/>

<br/>
</span>GENERAL OBSERVATIONS.<br/>
<br/>
The fare of <span style="color: #0000FF;">Â£47 </span>sterling, before mentioned, covers everything<br/>
--<span style="color: #0000FF;">living</span>, guides, donkeys, also saddles (English saddles for<br/>
