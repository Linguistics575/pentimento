<br/>
FIRST DAY.<br/>
<br/>
The <span style="color: #0000FF;">starting-place </span>is now from above the new iron bridge<br/>
"<span style="color: #0000FF;">Kaer-el-Nil</span>," which leads to the Pyramids of Ghizeh. The<br/>
Steamer generally leaves at 3 p.m., and arrives same evening at<br/>
<span style="color: #ff0000;">Badrachin </span>(railway station, and <span style="color: #0000FF;">starting-point </span>to visit the site<br/>
of Memphis, the Serapeum, and the Pyramids of <span style="color: #ff0000;">8alrksirah </span>and<br/>
<span style="color: #ff0000;">Dashbor</span>). Under the new arrangements, as worked out last<br/>
season, donkeys sent from Cairo the same day will be waiting at<br/>
<span style="color: #ff0000;">Badrachin </span>to take the passengers to Memphis, the Serapeum,<br/>
<span style="color: #ff0000;">850.</span>, before the departure of the Steamer the following morning.<br/>
These places, being so near to Cairo, and easily accessible by rail,<br/>
are generally visited from Cairo, donkeys being sent on during<br/>
the night, or taken along in the trains; <span style="color: #0000FF;">but </span>Steamers on their<br/>
way up the Nile now allow time for sight-seeing at this place.<br/>
<br/>
SECOND DAY.<br/>
<br/>
The excursion to <span style="color: #ff0000;">Sakkzirah </span>and the Serapeum is made, starting<br/>
in the morning. We have an opportunity on returning, and after<br/>
having visited the site where Memphis stood, to witness an important fair, held every Wednesday, at <span style="color: #ff0000;">a</span><span style="color: #ff0000;">, </span>village'on our way. We get<br/>
back at about noon to the Steamer, which starts immediately.<br/>
In the afternoon we pass on our right the curtailed Pyramid,<br/>
called "El <span style="color: #ff0000;">Kedib</span>," or False Pyramid. Towards evening we.<br/>
come to Waste, <span style="color: #0000FF;">a </span>village of no importance, where the Steamer<br/>
generally stops for the night, unless the night be clear and moonlit, in <span style="color: #0000FF;">which </span>case the Steamer continues its course for some time.<br/>
