ART. 3. Departure from <span style="color: #0000FF;">Stations</span><span style="color: #0000FF;">--</span><span style="color: #0000FF;">Half </span>an hour<br/>
before leaving any Station, the Captain of the Steamer will give<br/>

<br/>

duly registered and signed by all the other passengers.<br/>
<br/>
ART. 4. Interruption of <span style="color: #0000FF;">Voyage</span><span style="color: #0000FF;">--</span><span style="color: #0000FF;">Any </span><span style="color: #0000FF;">passenger<br/>
</span>wishing to leave the Steamer at any of the Intermediate Stations<br/>
may do <span style="color: #ff0000;">so</span><span style="color: #ff0000;">; </span>but, notwithstanding his having paid the full fare to<br/>
any other Station beyond, he will not be entitled to a passage<br/>
by any other Steamer, and forfeit the difference paid in favour of<br/>
the Administration.<br/>
<br/>
ART. 5. Arrival at <span style="color: #0000FF;">Cairo.-0n </span>reaching Cairo after the<br/>
expiration of the voyage, passengers will have to withdraw their<br/>
luggage immediately<span style="color: #ff0000;">; </span>twenty-four hours afterwards the luggage<br/>
will be landed at their expense, and deposited in <span style="color: #ff0000;">decks </span>of the<br/>
Administration, where the passengers may claim <span style="color: #0000FF;">it </span>on payment<br/>

<br/>

<br/>
ART. 8. In case any delay should arise to the Steamers, it<br/>
