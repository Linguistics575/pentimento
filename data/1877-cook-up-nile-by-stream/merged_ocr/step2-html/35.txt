of which, owing to the increasing <span style="color: #0000FF;">traffic </span>on the <span style="color: #ff0000;">Nile</span>, has been generally felt by<br/>
the travelling public.<br/>
<br/>
Previous to this new arrangement, no accommodation of any sort was to be<br/>

<br/>
The Luxor Hotel will be opened at the commencement of this season, and our<br/>

or down the Nile, thus affording opportunities for the antiquarian to visit<br/>
Karnac and Thebes, and to devote the time necessary for thoroughly examining<br/>
