ART. <span style="color: #0000FF;">I. </span><span style="color: #0000FF;">Tickets-Passage </span>Tickets may be had at the<br/>

<br/>
The Tickets are personal, and passengers holding the same are<br/>
entitled to be provided with food and one berth.<br/>
<br/>
Children from three to ten years of age pay half fares, viz.,<br/>
twenty Egyptian pounds, for the voyage to the First Cataract;<br/>
those under three years will go free, provided they do not exceed<br/>
their parents in number, in <span style="color: #0000FF;">which </span>case one-fourth of the passage<br/>
rate, viz., L. Egyp. 10, will be charged for each child.<br/>
<br/>
Passage Tickets for the First Cataract are available for<span style="color: #ff0000;">' </span>one<br/>
month from the date of delivery. For the Second Cataract,<br/>
fifteen days additional are allowed. Travellers not proceeding by<br/>
the first or second Steamer after taking their passage forfeit all<br/>
claim to any subsequent departure, and will have no claim against<br/>
the Administration for the passage money by them previously paid.<br/>
Should they proceed by the second Steamer, they will have to<br/>
be <span style="color: #0000FF;">satisfied </span>with any Cabin that may be given them, without any<br/>
claim to that mentioned on their Ticket.<br/>

<span style="color: #0000FF;">Ni-Any </span>person found on board the Steamers <span style="color: #0000FF;">without<br/>
