in 1865-70 that of the Kingdom of Italy, and now that of the<br/>

Venice as one of the most attractive towns in Italy. While in ancient<br/>
times Rome was the grand centre of Italian development, Florence<br/>
has since the middle ages superseded it as the focus of intellectual<br/>
life. The modern Italian language and literature have emanated<br/>
<span style="color: #0000FF;">chiefly </span>from <span style="color: #0000FF;">Florence </span><span style="color: #0000FF;">t </span>and the fine arts also attained the zenith of<br/>
their glory here. An amazing profusion of treasures of art, such as<br/>
no other locality possesses within so narrow limits, reminiscences<br/>
of a history which has influenced the whole of Europe, perpetuated<br/>
by numerous and imposing monuments, and lastly the <span style="color: #0000FF;">delightful<br/>
</span>environs of the city combine to render Florence one of the most<br/>
interesting and attractive places in the world.<br/>
