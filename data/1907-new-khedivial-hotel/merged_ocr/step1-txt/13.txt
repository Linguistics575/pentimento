Arab Village.

The greatest security prevails, not only in the town but
also in its immediate neighbourhood, and the crimes that
are frequent in the great capitals of Europe are extremely
rare here.

In fact, the fanaticism of the Moslem population is
infinitely less than in any other Mohammedan country,
and foreigners are very rarely insulted.
