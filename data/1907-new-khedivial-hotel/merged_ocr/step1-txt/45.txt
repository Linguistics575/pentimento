Notice.

All Guides and Camel.Drivers of the Pyramids must
have a license from the Moudirieh and wear a numbered
badge on the right arm.

They will be paid according to the following tariff:

P.T.
Ascent of Pyramids.. -..... .... IO
Visiting interior.... _,"...... IO
Visiting Ruins...... ........ 5
Ascent, visiting Interior and Ruins...... 20
Camel Hire, for one hour or fraction thereof.. 5
For the whole day...... ...... 30

Three Guides will always accompany each person
visiting the Interior or ascending the Pyramids. The
Sheiks will see that in giving information to visitors the
guides do not pester or annoy them. In case of complaints of this nature, Sheiks will be held responsible.
Visitors need not be accompanied by guides when visiting
the monuments and ruins. but they must not enter or
ascend the pyramids unattended.
