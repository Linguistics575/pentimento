The journey takes about two hours, through desert land<br/>
all the way. Bordering the line are stretches of marsh. To<br/>
the right are the hills, red- brown; to the left the Canal.<br/>
One can only see the canal momentarily where it appears,<br/>
blue as the curved blade of a Damascus sword. The only<br/>
sign of the waterway is the mast or funnel of some ship<br/>
gliding <span style="color: #ff0000;">slowly </span>from station to station.<br/>
