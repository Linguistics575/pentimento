INTRODUCTION OF FIREARMS AND AMMUNITION<br/>
INTO EGYPT<br/>
Persons desiring to bring rifles, shot-guns, or firearms into Egypt can do<br/>
so without a permit on condition that they are for their own private use, and<br/>
are not new.<br/>
If the firearms are new, though private, application for permits must be<br/>
made in writing to:<br/>
The Director of Artillery,<br/>
The Citadel, Cairo.<br/>
All arms will be subject to the usual Customs charges, <span style="color: #0000FF;">is</span>, <span style="color: #0000FF;">e </span>per cent. <span style="color: #0000FF;">on<br/>
</span>their value.<br/>

<span style="color: #ff0000;">tcheir </span>own use must first obtain a permit from the Ministry of the Interior<br/>
<span style="color: #0000FF;">aim</span>.<br/>
The permission necessary for the transport of this ammunition by railway is<br/>
