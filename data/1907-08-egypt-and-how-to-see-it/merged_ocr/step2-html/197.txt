SALOONS<br/>
Saloons can be attached to all trains provided they are disengaged. As long<br/>
notice as possible should be given beforehand to the station-master of the departure station, or to the Traffic Manager E.S.R. Cairo, who will furnish all particulars. _<br/>
<br/>
SALOONS BETWEEN LUXOR AND ASSOUAN<br/>
<br/>
Saloons, with or without <span style="color: #0000FF;">sleeping-berths</span>, may be hired between Luxor and<br/>
Assouan by parties not exceeding six persons. The price is LE3 in addition to<br/>
the ordinary first- class fares.<br/>
<br/>
Application for these should be made to the General Superintendent at Luxor<br/>
twenty-four hours in advance.<br/>
<br/>
SPECIAL TRAINS<br/>
<br/>
Applications for special trains must be addressed to the departure station at<br/>
least <span style="color: #0000FF;">five </span>hours in advance. The letter must state the destination, number of<br/>
passengers, class of carriages, number and description of animals to be transported, and approximate weight of the luggage.<br/>
<br/>
The price of a special train is calculated upon the number of <span style="color: #0000FF;">passengers </span>plus<br/>
20 per cent. of the ordinary fares. Animals and luggage are charged for at the<br/>
ordinary coaching <span style="color: #0000FF;">rates</span>.<br/>
<br/>
<span style="color: #0000FF;">The </span>minimum charge for the train is fixed at 200 <span style="color: #0000FF;">millieme </span>per kilometre,<br/>
with a minimum total charge of LE5 (five Egyptian pounds).<br/>
<br/>
Demurrage rates will be charged in accordance with the tariff.<br/>
<br/>

compartments.<br/>
<br/>

<br/>
Passengers may leave their luggage at the principal stations against receipt,<br/>
at the rate of 5 milliemes per parcel, and per twenty-four hours or portion<br/>
of twenty-four hours.<br/>
