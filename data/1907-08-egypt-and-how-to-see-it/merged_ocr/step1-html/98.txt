</span>LUXOR TO ASSOUAN<br/>
<br/>
At Luxor the line changes to narrow gauge, but the same<br/>
tradition of comfort is, as <span style="color: #0000FF;">far </span>as possible, maintained in the<br/>
trains; the windows are fitted with smoked glass to counteract the glare, and a <span style="color: #0000FF;">luncheon-car </span>is attached to the train.<br/>
<br/>
The journey takes six and a half hours.<br/>
<br/>
At Luxor station there is not only <span style="color: #0000FF;">a </span>buffet, but even baths<br/>
for the dusty.<br/>
<br/>
The best trains leave Luxor at 10.30 A.M. and Assouan <span style="color: #0000FF;">at<br/>
</span>10.15 A.M. in connection with the trains de <span style="color: #0000FF;">lame </span>and night<br/>
