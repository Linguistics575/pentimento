Cairo, as already stated, is a walled town, and possesses
71 gates i but the town has so increased since Saladin built
the walls, that many of the gates are far in the city. The
most important gates are the Bsib-el-Fotooh, or Gate of
Victory; the Bsib-el-Nasr, or Gate of Conquest; and the
Bab-el-Tooloon. By a staircase at the Béb-el-Nasr, the
visitor can ascend the wall, and walk along it to the Béb-el-
Fotooh.
