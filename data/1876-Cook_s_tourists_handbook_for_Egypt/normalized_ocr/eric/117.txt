C. Stelaef and Inscriptions.

Most of these are tablets taken from tombs, containing
the name, style, and titles of the deceased, offerings to the
gods, etc.

63. A celebrated stela from Thebes, containing an Egyptian
poem, composed thirty-five centuries ago, in praise of
Thothmes III.

72. On this stela is a Hymn to the Sun, which thus con-
cludes: -

"Grant that I may arrive at the land of eternity and the region of
those who are approved; grant that I may be re-united to the excellent

and wise spirits of Ker-neter; and that I may appear with them to
contemplate thy beauties in the morning of every day."
