and healthy winter residence. In December and January the<br/>
whole of the Northern Sudan is at its best; the Nile is still<br/>
high, and the river shows to the greatest advantage. January is<br/>
the coolest month. The Grand Hotel, in which Thos. Cook &<br/>
Son, Ltd., have a Branch Office, is under the management of<br/>
