a series of rapids, <span style="color: #0000FF;">ever </span><span style="color: #0000FF;">make </span>of graywacke, greenstone, and granite,<br/>
forming numerous islands in its course. The best point of view is<br/>
the abrupt rocky <span style="color: #0000FF;">hill </span>of <span style="color: #0000FF;">Abusir</span>, on the W. bank. At the foot of the<br/>
hill, contrasting strongly with the green of the tamarisk-trees<span style="color: #ff0000;">, </span>lies<br/>
<span style="color: #0000FF;">a </span>chaos of glistening black boulders, through which the river forces<br/>
