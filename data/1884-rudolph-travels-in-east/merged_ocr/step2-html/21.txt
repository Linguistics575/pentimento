MY Eastern tour had been quickly decided upon, and<br/>
the preparations for it were made with speed. Various<br/>
books of Eastern travel were rapidly looked through, and a<br/>
few days before our start the luggage, a skin-dresser with<br/>
all his apparatus, the servants, guns, plenty of ammunition,<br/>
and several dogs, were sent off to Trieste, where the yacht<br/>
<span style="color: #0000FF;">Miramar</span>, which had graciously been placed at our disposal,<br/>
awaited us<span style="color: #ff0000;">.<br/>
