</span>TO <span style="color: #ff0000;">ABUSKAR</span>--<span style="color: #ff0000;">Jormsw </span><span style="color: #0000FF;">TO </span>SIUT.<br/>
<br/>
EARLY on the morning of the 23rd February the whole<br/>
travelling party gathered in the station of that southern<br/>
line of railway which leads, not only to Siut, but also has a<br/>
branch into the province of Fayum. Besides ourselves, the<br/>
two brothers Saurma also put in an appearance.<br/>
<br/>
Herr Zimmerman had again the goodness to conduct<br/>
our train, and to accompany us to the last station, <span style="color: #ff0000;">Abuskar</span>.<br/>
Prince Taxis had started the day before, with a dragoman,<br/>
for the lake of Birket-el-Karun, to pitch our tents and<br/>
arrange the hunting days<span style="color: #ff0000;">.<br/>
